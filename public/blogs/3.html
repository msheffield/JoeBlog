Dec. 21, 2016

So since I am writing my first entry towards the end of December, I figured I would do a long and rambling recap of my 2016 and what I'm looking forward to about 2017. This year has been pretty crazy

It began last December. I loaded up my life in the bed of my truck, said goodbye to a ton of great friends, and moved from Houston to Seattle.
