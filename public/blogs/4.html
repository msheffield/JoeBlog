2017 Is Starting Off With A Bang

Feb. 26, 2017

Well this blog is going to take a sharp turn in 2017. The good news is I found a job! I was offered a position as a Full Stack Developer with The Spry Group. I am really happy with this work and love the projects I have been working on.

This last Wednesday, unfortunately, I received some pretty shitty news. I was diagnosed with esophageal cancer. This is a bad diagnosis. I don’t know much yet but right now it looks inoperable. I have a meeting tomorrow, Monday, with a specialist at the Seattle Cancer Care Alliance to discuss other treatment options.

In the last few days, I have received the most amazing support from all my friends and family. I cannot put into words what each text, call, or visit has meant to me. Thank you so much for your messages and your prayers. Because sometimes I might not be able to return every text and call, I would like to use this blog as a place for me to give the people I love updates on my treatment and experiences with this disease. I love every one of you and I you all mean the world to me. I am really lucky to have such a strong support system, as well as two physician parents who can advocate for my treatment. This would be even scarier to be going through alone.

Thanks for reading. I’m not giving up. Hopefully I can get this blog back to the tech/jobsearch/lifestyle blog I originally intended it to be.

Love, Joe
