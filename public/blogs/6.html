I Got an Implant

March 10, 2017

Cancer update (and stuff) #2

A better week for me. Last Saturday, I had a minor surgical procedure at the UW Hospital. I had a power port slipped under the skin in my chest with a catheter that sits in a vein. This is great! Now they don't have to stick my arms every time they want to draw blood or give me chemo, they can just access this port and I can hide any tubes under my shirt if I'm out and about. Here I am about to get it, very excited!

My cousin Dan and his girlfriend Rachel drove up from Portland for a visit. It is always great to see them. He opened me up to something called Mindfulness Based Stress Reduction. I brought it up with my mom and she said that it had helped her while she was fighting breast cancer. Anyways, Cristina and I are both signed up to take the class, which will start next week. I am excited to get my body AND mind as healthy as possible while I take all these crummy drugs to try and kill this cancer. Thanks Dan and Mom and Dad!

This week has me missing my Houston fam as well as it is the start of the St. Patrick's Day festivities at Griff's! When I lived in Houston, the people that I met there really made me feel like I had a family when I was so far away from my blood fam.
